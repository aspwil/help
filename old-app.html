find old google playstore apps:

ever been trying to find an old app you lost?
well i have a solution give you meet a few conditions:
- you installed it though the google playstore
- you still have access to the google account you installed it from

here is how:
- on a phone logged into the relevant google account go into the play store app
- click on the user icon in the top right
- click on "manage apps and devices"
- at the top click the tab labeled "Manage"
- in the first drop down menu at the top labeled "Installed" click it and set it to "Not Installed"

this will pull up a list of every app you have installed though that google account, that you don't currently have installed on the phone.

now the annoying part, you can sort by alphabetical, or by when you last had it installed, you cant search by name though.

now simply scroll though the list of possibly thousands of apps and see if you can find the one your looking for.

annoying, but it can work if your running out of other options.
